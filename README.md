# European-Bank-Customer-Churn-Analysis
European Bank Customer Churn Analysis using SQL, Python, Power BI, and Streamlit to identify customer churn patterns and business insights.
## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer churn in a European bank using SQL, Python, Power BI, and Streamlit. The objective is to identify the factors influencing customer churn and provide actionable business insights to improve customer retention.

##  Objectives

* Analyze customer churn patterns.
* Identify high-risk customer segments.
* Compare churn rates across different countries.
* Study the impact of customer engagement and financial behavior.
* Build interactive dashboards for business decision-making.

##  Key Business Insights

* Germany has the highest customer churn rate.
* Customers aged 30–60 are more likely to churn.
* Inactive customers have a significantly higher churn rate.
* Customers with higher balances are more likely to leave.
* Customer engagement plays a major role in reducing churn.
